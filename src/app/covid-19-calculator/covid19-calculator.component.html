What is this calculator ?
* This project is not the part of this website, I have incorporated the calculator to make people aware and to be informed, But we have to take precaution no matter what this calculator predicts or say.
Hope everyone is safe and doing their best because that's what is demanded from the humanity right now.
This project was created and managed by the data research team at Nexoid. Nexoid is a software company located in London, United Kingdom. We have skills in research, analysis, and computer science. We are not medical professionals.
The service we provide is based on a changing statistical model. This model is continuously being updated to produce the most accurate predictions possible. If you want to know more about how the model works, please go to the research section of this website.

About calculator:
  • Filling in the calculator gives you an insight into your own personal risk as well as providing data that will assist researchers around the world.
  • We have made the raw data available for download. The more people analyzing and working with this dataset, the more likely we are to discover something that will save lives.

  • Data and privacy: This calculator estimates your risk of infection and mortality from COVID-19. To do this we use the data you are about to enter on this page and your location (derived from your IP address). There is no other data being collected.
  • The data collected is added to a free dataset which can be downloaded by anyone from this website. The dataset is critical for researchers to understand and fight COVID-19. Your data will help save lives.
