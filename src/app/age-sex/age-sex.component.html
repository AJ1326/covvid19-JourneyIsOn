COVID-19 Fatality Rate by AGE

This probability differs depending on the age group. The percentages shown below do not have to add up to 100%, as they do NOT represent share of deaths by age group. Rather, it represents, for a person in a given age group, the risk of dying if infected with COVID-19.

Death Rate = (number of deaths / number of cases) = probability of dying if infected by the virus (%).
